---
question: "Can I park near the fair?"
order: 3
---

Gledhow Valley Road is car-free on the day of the fair, so there is no parking on the road itself.

Limited residential parking is available in the surrounding streets, but please be considerate of local residents and use public transport where possible. Bus routes serving the area include 12, 13, 13A, 874, X98, X99 (via Roundhay Road) and 2, 3, 3A, 91, 36 (via Harrogate Road).
