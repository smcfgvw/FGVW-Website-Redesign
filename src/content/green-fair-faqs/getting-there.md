---
question: "How do I get to the Green Fair?"
order: 1
---

The fair is on Gledhow Valley Road, which will be car-free on the day. We strongly encourage you to travel by public transport, bicycle, or on foot.

**By bus:**
- Routes 12, 13, 13A, 874, X98, X99 stop on Roundhay Road (east end)
- Routes 2, 3, 3A, 91, 36 stop on Harrogate Road (west end)

Both ends of Gledhow Valley Road are a short walk from bus stops on these routes.
