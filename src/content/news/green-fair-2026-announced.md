We are delighted to announce that the Gledhow Valley Green Fair will return on **Sunday 12 July 2026**, from 11am to 4pm.

As in previous years, Gledhow Valley Road will be car-free for the day, creating a safe and welcoming space for the whole community to enjoy.

The fair is free to attend and is suitable for all ages. It celebrates the woods, the valley, and the wider community that makes this place so special.

Look out for information about stalls, activities, entertainment, and how to get involved — as a volunteer, exhibitor, or sponsor — coming soon.

Follow us on Facebook and Instagram @GledhowValleyGreenFair for updates.
