Gledhow Beck continues to be affected by pollution from misconnected drains and sewage discharges in the catchment above the valley. As our [water testing programme](/water-testing) has shown, this has a real impact on the health of the beck and the wildlife that depends on it.

We've launched a petition calling for action to stop water pollution from misconnections in the Gledhow Valley catchment, and we'd be grateful for your support.

**[Sign the petition on Change.org →](https://www.change.org/p/stop-water-pollution-from-misconnections-in-the-gledhow-valley?recruiter=642749081&recruited_by_id=0c466b30-b425-11e6-bdec-9b64f9cca636&redirect_reason=deprecated-url)**

Every signature helps demonstrate the strength of public feeling and supports our case for action from the relevant authorities. Please also share the petition with friends, family, and neighbours who care about the valley.
