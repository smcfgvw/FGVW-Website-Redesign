Our regular volunteer work parties are a great way to get involved with practical conservation in the woods. This session will focus on maintaining the paths alongside Gledhow Beck and clearing vegetation from the lakeside margins.

No experience needed — all tools and guidance provided. Just bring sturdy footwear, waterproof clothing, and gloves if you have them.

Work parties usually run for around three hours and are followed by a well-earned cup of tea.
