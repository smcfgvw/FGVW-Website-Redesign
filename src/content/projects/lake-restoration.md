---
title: "Gledhow Lake Restoration"
status: completed
description: "A major project to restore Gledhow Lake — removing accumulated silt, reinforcing paths, and improving biodiversity. Funded by the National Lottery Heritage Fund."
---

Gledhow Lake, the medieval lake beside Gledhow Valley Road, was restored in 2019–2020 with the support of a £50,000 National Lottery Heritage Fund grant.

The project involved removing substantial quantities of accumulated silt from the lake bed, reinforcing the paths around the margins, and undertaking planting and habitat improvements to increase biodiversity.

The lake is now in significantly better condition and provides improved habitat for the wide range of wildlife that depends on it.
