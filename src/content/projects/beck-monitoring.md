title: "Gledhow Beck Water Quality Monitoring"
status: active
description: "Ongoing citizen science project monitoring water quality in Gledhow Beck, Leeds's urban watercourse running through the valley."
---

Since 2018, FGVW volunteers have been carrying out regular water quality testing of Gledhow Beck as part of our citizen science programme.

Testing takes place monthly and measures key indicators including phosphate levels, nitrates, pH, biological oxygen demand, and the presence of indicator invertebrates.

Results are reported to Leeds City Council and shared with partner organisations including REAP (Responsible, Ethical and Accountable Procurement).
