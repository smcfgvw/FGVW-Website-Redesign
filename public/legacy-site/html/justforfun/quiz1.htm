What Do You Call a Lot of...?

Did you know that when there are a lot of sparrows together they're known as a "host of sparrows"? If you did, why not test your knowledge on some other creatures you might find gathering in Gledhow Valley Woods.

Instructions

Just click on a type of creature, then click on the word which can be used to mean a group of those creatures. Each time you match a pair correctly your score increases, but if you get one wrong you'll have to start again. To start from the beginning, click on the "Reset" button. Good luck...

Note: Please wait until the whole web page has fully loaded before starting, or the game might not work properly!
