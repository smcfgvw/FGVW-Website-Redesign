Become an FGVW Voting Member

The purchase of a meadow in 2016 required FGVW to become a registered charity. Following on from this, paid membership has been introduced for supporters wishing to vote at AGMs, become an officer or stand as a trustee.

Please Note: Membership is not needed to attend Action Mornings, Steering Group Meetings or AGMs, or to receive FGVW Update e-mails.

Membership is only needed if you want to vote at AGMs, become a officer or stand as a trustee.
Membership is £5 per year or £50 for life membership.

To become a voting FGVW Member, please see our current contact details for how to get in touch with the Membership Secretary.
