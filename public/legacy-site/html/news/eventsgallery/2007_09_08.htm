8th-9th September 2007
Gipton Spa Bath House Heritage Open Days

As part of Leeds Heritage Open Days, the Gipton Spa Bath House on Gledhow Valley Road was opened to the public under the supervision of FGVW members on Saturday 8th and Sunday 9th September 2007 (2.00-4.00pm). On the Sunday there was also a 45 minute "Herb Walk", led by local herbalist Donald Purves.

The open days were once again very popular, with hundreds of people turning up to find out about the building and take the guided tour. Once again we were lucky enough to have sunny weather most of the time. Many people left comments in the Visitor's Book, and these can be seen at the bottom of this page. You can also find out more about the bath house in the About the Woods section.

The event also helped increase general interest in the area by way of information displays and fact sheets. The Group was also able to raise money from the sale of merchandise, a "Hunt the Gledhow Valley Bear" game, and from generous visitor donations.

Our thanks go to everyone who helped on the days, those who provided the home made refreshments, and everyone who came to learn a bit about the history of this little gem of a building.
