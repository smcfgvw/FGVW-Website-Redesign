Chapel Allerton Open Gardens Sunday 11 June 2017 (1.00-5.00pm)
Location: A walkable trail spanning Allerton Park, Gledhow Park Road, Montreal Avenue, King George Avenue and a few in between.
Chapel Allerton gardeners invite you into their gardens, off the beaten track, to share ideas and sneak a peak at the wonderful views of Gledhow Valley. Produce may be available to buy at some gardens. All proceeds to St Gemma's Hospice and Friends of Gledhow Valley Woods. Maps will be available at community events in the run up to the event and on the website. All open gardens will be identified by balloons on gates.

General Meeting* Friday 24 March 2017 (7.30pm)
Three Hulats Pub, 13 Harrogate Road, Chapel Allerton
This replaces the normal Annual General Meeting of previous years as the first official AGM of the new CIO is not due to be held until the period 1 January 2018 - 31 March 2018, to accord with Charity Commission requirements. As a consequence, the main business of the meeting will be the election of new trustees, although the final accounts for FGVW as an unincorporated organisation for the period up to 10 October 2016 (the day preceding the date of incorporation) will be presented along with the Chair's Report on activity during the year. We hope to see you there.
