8. Heritage Lottery Fund Project Update

David M is looking at phase 2 of the project. Nick Hartley is a contractor who helped with phase 1. Adrian, Steve and David met with Nick and did a walk around the woods and beck area. Silt is building up again and forming an island that is slowing the water down further and causing even more silt to build up. A suggestion was to plant the new island up and put in a solar powered aerating system to help move the silt along and break down the leaves quicker. A peninsular could be created to cause a faster water current which would help take the silt out into the deep water. Nick will come back with ideas and costings so we can look at raising the funding.

Also, there was a meeting with Yorkshire Wildlife to look at improving the wildlife and planting in the beck and wet areas. A proposal was made to add in reed beds near the CSO area but its not clear who owns the land.

9. FGVW Sales Items

Adrian is arranging for an engineer from Yorkshire Water to look at the area around the valley and in particular cross connections and sewage overflows. There was a suggestion for regular review meetings with the initial meeting expected on a Wednesday.

13. AOB

  • Mervyn has been in contact with Chris Salt and walked around the woods looking at the sewage blockage in the meadow and with a view to looking at other sewage problems and CSOs.
  • David told the Group about Space 2 in east Leeds who are putting on a play (called "East Side Story") about the east side of Leeds and Wyke Beck, which flows into the River Aire. They want to film the Bath House as part of this. The Group agreed and Mervyn can supply the keys. They are also doing 4 events at Bracken Woods Community Centre and are looking for volunteers to go and speak to them about the woods. They aim to put on a show at the Playhouse in Leeds in July. Adrian volunteered to speak, and the Group wondered if this was something Michael would be interested in doing. Volunteers are needed on Tuesday 28th June and 8th July 11-11.30am.
  • Carol has successfully applied for 120 hedge saplings to be delivered to the Group in November from the Woodlands Trust as part of the Queens Jubilee Green Canopy Project. These will be used in Beck Pasture as suggested by Joan and Mervyn.
  • Adrian has applied to the Co-Op Community Fund again on behalf of the Group.
