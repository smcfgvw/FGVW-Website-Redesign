6. Groundwork Application

The application is going forward. Groundwork have sent a map of the woods so that the Group can look at putting in up to 10 benches. The Group discussed where they should go and proposed just putting in 4-6 benches and utilising the rest of the money for another project. Members of the Group are going to conduct a site visit and decide the best places for the benches.

Francesca Jenkinson has also been in contact with Adam over the information boards. Adam will supply the information he has already collated for her to use. The Group may add some boards of their own depending on how well these boards survive. Initially 3 boards will be put up at the entrances being developed under the agreed application.

7. Roundhay Live

There is a large REAP event taking place on Saturday 18th June 2011. It is a chance for all local community groups to get together to promote the "sustainable community". Between 25 and 30 groups are expected to attend.

The day starts at 1pm for picnics with the main event open form 2pm to 4pm on the Soldiers' Field opposite the Oakwood Clock. There will be lots of activities taking place such as sports, hobbies, art, music, local history, environment and demonstrations by the various groups. Everyone is welcome to attend.

The Group agreed to loan marquees and pop up gazebos. More volunteers are needed to help on the day.
