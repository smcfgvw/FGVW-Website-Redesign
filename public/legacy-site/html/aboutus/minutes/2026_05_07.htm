FCC community fund application deadline mid august. LCC parks and countryside have to submit the application which is a benefit for us. Awaiting response on 2 other requests. Postcode lottery meet 1st June. Womble Dixon. Lawyers who are also interested in supporting us. The project will focus on 3 areas. Initial focus is the allerton grange way. Bracken wood community association are also keen to be involved to see how they can support the project.

9. Water Quality Monitoring

We've decided to join the sewage campaign network. A group of like minded water quality groups with a common goal Have started network meetings on June 1st. We had a meeting with council highway dept on Monday. To discuss how silt is running into the beck. A supporter doing a PhD in roadside gullies. We're aware that Zinc and lead levels are very high. Gully on allerton grange way is quite poor. Has been reported to env agency. We've done a freedom of info request to Env Agency and Yorkshire Water to see what action they've taken.

Josh raised an idea of an artist who looks to use art to raise the visibility of issues like this.

We have over 3000 signatures via Facebook on our water quality issues.
