7. AOB

  • Probation Workers
    The probation workers have done some excellent work in the woods and clearing the pavements. Mike is going to write to Brenda Lancaster to thank them on behalf of the Group.
  • St Matthew's Summer Fair
    The St Matthew's Summer Fair has been cancelled. The Group has been invited to their Strawberry Fair on 17 July instead.
  • 3 Hulats' Farmers Market
    The Group has been asked if they would like to attend on 30 May.
  • Swans on the Lake
    A pair of swans has returned to the lake and are nesting.
  • Drainage
    There is a problem with the small drain built under the path near the lake by the Council. It is three quarters full of silt and the Group are considering clearing it and maybe putting a cover over it before it blocks up completely and damages the path.
  • Wooden Barriers
    The wooden barriers at the junction of Gledhow Valley Road and Allerton Grange have been badly damaged. Joan has contacted the Council who has repaired some in the area but not these. She will chase them up.
  • Japanese Knotweed
    The Japanese Knotweed has returned and is a lot worse this year. The Group agreed to contact the Council to see if it could be sprayed again.
  • FGVW Funds
    Paul has asked if some of the £4,000 in the current account can be moved into the savings account. The Group decided to wait until after the FunDay in case money is needed due to lack of grants.
