10. Any Other Business

  • Christmas Cards
    Christmas Card sales have been slow since the Heritage Open Day. Adam has contacted Radish, who may be interested, but not until nearer Christmas. Dorothy has agreed to try and sell some at the local church Christmas Fayre.

  • New Development?
    Joan informed the Group that a notice board had been put up concerning some development work near to the old car park area. The Group are continuing to look for any planning signs or development.

  • Water Levels in the Lake
    Joan and Mervyn raised concerns over the water levels in the lake over the past week. At one stage the lake was overflowing; a drain had been forced up by the water and was overflowing but the CSO was working OK. By the 27th the water levels had reduced substantially so Joan contacted Joe Smallman to find out more information.
    Allegedly someone had deliberately blocked the drains so that the lake was overflowing. This was potentially hazardous as it could have led to flooding in the valley and surrounding homes. The Council removed the blockage but also had to drain the lake to get at the machinery to remove the obstruction in the drain. Joe stated that the lake should fill up again to normal level.
    It is believed that the Council (Learning Leisure) and the local police are aware of the person who is continually causing damage to the lake and deliberately altering the water levels. The Group is frustrated that nothing is being done to stop him and concerned that he could cause floods that will damage local residents property.
    Mervyn suggested that the Group make a list of all the damage that has been done, the consequences and the cost of repairs by the Council, which is running into thousands and then visit the surrounding houses to make local residents aware.
    Joan agreed to contact Avril, a local resident who was a victim of flooding earlier in the year and who was asked to give her story at the last Scrutiny Board meeting for an update.
    Adrian suggested asking the Community Constables to speak to the Group about what they are doing to stop this person.
