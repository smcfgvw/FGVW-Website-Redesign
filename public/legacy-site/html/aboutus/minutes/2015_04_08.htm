6. Ranger Attendance/Stewardship

The Group discussed the Stewardship scheme with Emma Trickett a couple of years ago. It was rejected awaiting further information and it didn't look like there would be any benefit to the Group. Responsibility would just switch form the Council to the Group. Martin discussed this with Woodhouse Ridge as they have a Stewardship arrangement already. It costs them an extra £200 per year for insurance. The Group agreed that more information was still necessary especially how it would be split between the Council, Wade's Charity and Housing & Leisure. On paper it looks like the Group will take on a lot of new obligations with no benefits. Martin agreed to talk to Jenny Rodgers.

7. Audrey's Meadow/Green Leeds

The Green Leeds panel sits on 21st April 2015. Our application has gone in so we will find out the results later this month. Last month the Council rotivated the meadow and we are going to get it sprayed to deter growth so we can spread the seeds and put plug plants in. We have two areas that were rotivated earlier. David Blackwell will continue to spray the second area but the first area will be planted up 3 weeks after spraying if we get the grant.

9. AOB

  • Pavement Issues
    Martin to e-mail the Councillors on the path work where a gully has been put in to stop the area flooding.
  • Grass Verge Issues
    Mike reported that the grass verge has been churned up and cars are getting stuck. It will need to be reported to Highways.
  • Holes in the Meadow
    Mike stated that "Martin's Meadow" can't be walked on as it is full of holes with grass covering them. He asked if we could cut a path through as in previous years.
  • Brackenwoods Fly-tipping
    Geoff fed back to the Group that he was getting nowhere with clearing the Brackenwoods area. He has e-mailed the Councillors but no received no response or commitment. Lincombe Drive end is still having household rubbish dumped there. Peter suggested serving a litter abatement notice on the Council. We need to collect evidence and send photos to serve the notice.
  • Free Pot Plant Plant Pots!
    Joan told the Group that some PCSOs had called to see if the Group wanted any plant pots they have taken from a cannabis raid. This is part of the Proceeds of Crime initiative. The article and photos will be used in the police magazine.
  • Building Work Concerns
    Mervyn and Geoff expressed concerns over the safety of some local building work. Builders have expanded Brackenwood Close and dug away behind the house. It is being held back but it looks unstable and dangerous.
