3. Matters Arising

  • Benches
    Averil would like benches putting in at the top of the garden in return for using her water and electricity for cleaning the Bath House. It is difficult to access so Martin and Mervyn assessed the situation and have decided to order some lighter benches. These will arrive at the beginning of June and will be put in at a convenient time.
  • Pavement Gully
    A gully stretches across the pavement in an area that has been flooding. Martin is arranging to meet with Councillor Ully to discuss the issue.
  • Fly Tipping
    There is an ongoing issue with fly tipping in the Brackenwoods area. Someone is emptying black bin liners of household rubbish into the woods. A team from the Housing Department came out to see what the problem was. Some of the flat easy to access area has been cleared but not the hilly area. It is getting problematic and one of the members is going to issue a litter abatement order on the Council. There was some concern that the situation will get worse when the Council change the frequency of emptying bins to fortnightly. There has also been another article in the local Press stating how Councillors are helping with litter clearing!

4. Correspondence

Received in relation to the fly tipping problem, as above.

7. Ranger Attendance/Stewardship

The Group discussed the Stewardship scheme with Emma Trickett a couple of years ago. It was rejected pending further information and it didn't look like there would be any benefit to the Group. Certain responsibilities would just switch form the Council to the Group. Martin and Mervyn are going to do a walkabout with Dan and Justin from the Council to discuss the work that will need doing next year and will also discuss the Stewardship with them and what we will need to do. It will be put on the agenda to discuss after the FunDay.

8. Audrey's Meadow/Green Leeds

The Group applied for a Green Leeds grant but it has been put on hold. They will consider giving us half the money if the Group can match it as well as obtaining more letters of support from the Community. Adrian to ask for support at the FunDay and Adam to e-mail members. The meadow has been sprayed and if we want to plant it later in the year we will need a grant. The meadow will be sprayed a couple more times before then. It may also need rotivating. The Group will re-apply for the grant at the end of June when it has (hopefully) received more support.
