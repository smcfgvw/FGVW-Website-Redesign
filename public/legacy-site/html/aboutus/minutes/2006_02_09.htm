5. Action Mornings

The next Action Morning is Saturday 11th February. The Group plan to use the strimmer on the meadow and cut back the brambles in the meadow and near the lake.

Action Morning Attendance Plan

The Group agreed it would be easier to promote the Action Mornings if a full programme of future dates were arranged. This has now been done.

Adam has looked into printing FGVW information leaflets on recycled paper. Adam designed a leaflet for comments and Paul asked if the details concerning FGVW could fill one leaflet panel and the web site details and merchandise on another panel. Martin suggested making the alterations and getting some printed in time for the FunDay and the Bath House Open Days. It was also suggested to add a flyer to the leaflets advertising upcoming Action Morning dates. Adrian also agreed to look into advertising Action Morning dates in a local Chapel Allerton newspaper.

The Group discussed ways of making the posters advertising the action mornings more visible. Adam suggested removing the posters straight after the action morning so that people didn't get used to their being a poster there all the time. It was also suggested making the lettering larger to grab peoples' attention.

Action Morning Activities Plan

The Group discussed what activities should be done at future Action Mornings and decide what people would like to do each month. Future ideas included working on the path near the bench with the nettles as it is getting muddy and slippery, to keep the paths in good order and do general maintenance on them, clear the vegetation along the side of the paths and also work on the path that the school children use towards Bracken Woods. The Group decided to plan the June Action Morning so that it can be advertised at the FunDay. Work on the footpath was proposed.
