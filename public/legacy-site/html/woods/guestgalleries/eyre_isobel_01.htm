1991 - Dredging Gledhow Lake
by Isobel Eyre

These photographs, taken by Isobel Eyre, show Gledhow Lake being dredged by Leeds City Council. Dredging is carried out to clear the lake of accumulated silt and debris and is done roughly every 15-20 years.
